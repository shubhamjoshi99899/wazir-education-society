What is "Retire a Device" initiative?"

India is fast-paced when it comes to technology. At Wazir Education Society, we try to coordinate with technology and keep it in mind when we teach the kids. Computers are an important part of the curriculum. We feel that these children need to understand computers properly if they want to be employable in future. .
