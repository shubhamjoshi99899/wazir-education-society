Wazir Education Society

Wazir Education Society (WES) is India's one of the trusted NGO working towards the Empowerment & Education of Children from Rural India. Our Top-most priority & goal is Children.

It is the 21st Century, The World has excelled itself with technologies and English is the Universal tongue. Yet to our dismay, at least 35 million children aged between 6-15 can not attend schools in India, Primary Education is barred from children who happen to be the future of India.

Therefore, WES believes that whether you are addressing healthcare, poverty, unemployment or human rights there's no better place to start than in the corridors of education.

We at WES have taken up the aim to educate the underpriviledged children on the two main topics; English & Computer.
