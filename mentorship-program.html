Mentorship Program

Mentorship Programme.

Mentorship Programme is an initiative started by Wazir Education Society on 14th July, 2022 with the aim to bring students in contact with Mentors, who can guide and motivate them at every point of their academic life. WES takes part in introducing Students & Mentors to one another for the Mentorship Programme.

Mentorship ProgrammeThe Mentorship Programme allows individuals from all spheres of life, whether university student, or professionals to bring a change, and to impact a students life so they can believe that education can make this World a better place. Mentors create a footprint for the students by encouraging them to focus on their academics & by developing their career plan envisioning a better and brighter future like an elder sibling.

We at WES believe that Mentors play a pivotal role for the students in identifying potential career opportunities, whereas to develop study plans and work on their career goals.
