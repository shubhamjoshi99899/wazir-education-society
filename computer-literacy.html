Computer Literacy at Schools

Computer Literacy at Schools.

The Computer Literacy at School is an educational & fun way Wazir Education Society (WES) teaches the students of the government primary schools in tribal areas, and makes them understand the basic Mathematics, English, Hindi & Computers.

The classes are held daily from Monday to Friday, with 1 hour interactive classes for each subject. Our Teachers from WES go to Government Primary School with laptops, and schedule classes. The teaching is done in a very light environment, which initiates with Maths, English & Hindi being taught by rhyming videos. Further, The fundamentals of computer is taught, small clips are shown and it is followed by Question & Answer Sessions. To conclude, The best performing students get to play video games on the computer as a driving force for motivation.

The aim behind the Computer Literacy sessions by WES are, primarily the introduction of computer to Tribal Kids so as to increase their literacy, also to motivate them to attend school regularly. Besides, teaching them in a fun driven way, and putting no performance pressure by evaluating. The main objective is to build within them Employable skills to as to build a global connect as well as to reduce child labour.

The Need.

  • To introduce tribal kids to computers.
  • To enhance computer literacy.
  • To improve school attendance rate.
  • To provide reachable fun driven learning.
  • To develop employable skills with global connect.
  • No performance pressure as no evaluation.
  • To reduce child labor

What we do.

  • Our Teachers go to government primary schools with a laptop and make a class of students.
  • They start by teaching them Maths, English, Hindi using rhyming videos for the first 10 minutes.
  • For the next 20 minutes they teach fundamentals of computers showing small video clips.
  • This is followed by a question & answer session .
  • The best performing students get to play Video Games for 20 minutes to keep them motivated and make the class more fun driven.
